Garlic Mushroom

A spectacular mix of smoky, earthy, and umami — mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 pound (500 g) brown mushrooms, sliced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon fresh cracked black pepper
  • 1/2 cup (125 ml) vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Heat oil and butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat until butter has melted. Sauté the mushrooms while stirring occasionally, until mushrooms are golden (about 5-6 minutes).
  2. Stir in garlic, thyme, parsley, salt and pepper; sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the broth and lemon juice. Cook, while stirring occasionally, until liquid has reduced slightly (about 3-4 minutes).
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve immediately.
